What We Do

Publications and Data Sets

Admission Discharge Transfer (ADT)

Patient transfers and location within hospital

Earliest data: February 1, 2020

Alberta Continuing Care Information System (ACCIS)

Continuing care stays including long-term care (LTC), designated supportive living (DSL) and home-living (HL)

Earliest data: January 1, 2012

Connect Care

Connect Care is the provincial electronic medical record.

Earliest data: November 3, 2019

COVID-19 Data (CDOM and PROVLAB)

PROVLAB captures COVID testing data in Alberta while CDOM captures public health assessments and contact tracing.

Earliest data: February 1, 2020

Diagnostic Imaging

Diagnostic images performed in AHS hospital based facilities

Earliest data: January 1, 2011

Discharge Abstract Database (DAD)

Acute care inpatient hospitalizations in Alberta

Earliest data: April 1, 2002

Emergency Department Information System (EDIS)

Ambulatory encounters in Alberta, including emergency department, same-day surgery, and day procedures.

Earliest data: April 1, 2010

Laboratory

All AHS general lab tests including clinical chemistry, toxicology, hematology, serology, urinalysis and immunology.

Earliest data: April 1, 2012

National Ambulatory Care Reporting System (NACRS)

Ambulatory encounters in Alberta, including emergency department, same-day surgery, and day procedures.

Earliest data: April 1, 2002

Pharmaceutical Information Network (PIN)

Prescription drugs dispensed from most community pharmacies in Alberta.

Earliest data: April 1, 2008

Practitioner Claims

Health service claims submitted for payment by providers in Alberta under the Alberta Health Care Insurance Plan.

Earliest data: April 1, 1994

Provincial Registry

Identifies whether people have coverage with Alberta Health Care Insurance Plan (AHCIP) and can be used to identify deaths for Alberta residents.

Earliest data: April 1, 1994

Vital Statistics

Identifies dates of death for Alberta residents

Earliest data: January 1, 1999
